Our Vision

Female art has been denied its place in history for far too long. With the emergence of Web3 and their open NFT art fairs, a unique new construction to express yourself got established.

It allows any person - independent of gender, social, and ethnic background - to express its uniqueness in an open, global space. Outside of the homogenous and restricted boundary of existing art galleries.

Poppypix collections have the intent to express diversity with their NFT's. While there are infinite differences in humans to make them individuals, most of us subconsciously define diversity by a few social categories.

A poppypix NFT aims to express diversity by applying traits that may resonate core beliefs and values of a potential owner.

They are all hand-crafted by a woman artist and may be seen as a new, peaceful and soft NFT art expressing diversity.
